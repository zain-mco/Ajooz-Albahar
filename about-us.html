About Us

Ajooz Albahar Seafood Restaurant

We believe that our customers is the most valuable part in our business, we are here to support you and to make every possible things to make a unique value for you. Not only with our fantastic combination of dishes, but also our incomparable services, Prices & our creative dishes which you cannot find anywhere else! You can rely on our experts and trust them to give you all information you need, and to make the most trusted recommendations for your meal, in that way that you can’t find ever better.

Ajooz Albahar Restaurant offers an extensive variety of HOURLY fresh seafood. Ajooz Albahar specialized dishes include Local, International and Thai Cuisines ,as well as our unique VIP Fish Boats and Private huge seafood trays, which can cater more than 40 persons in one tray, with more than 35 kg of premium Seafood varieties.Guests can select from the Big and Wonderful variety of seafood Display, with a flexible option to be prepared to their taste with recommended flavours of the Master Chef.

Ajooz Albahar Seafood Restaurant has a Staple strategy, which is to get our freshly caught seafood selections on hourly basis from Abu Dhabi Fresh Seafood Port, Never allow any remaining seafood stock to be served for customers from a day to another. Our Seafood selections vary from Sheri, King Fish, Grey Mullet, Local Hamour, Sultan Ibrahim, Pomfret, Jumbo & Super Jumbo Shrimps, Crabs, Lobsters, Lady Finger, Calamari, Cuttlefish, Clams, Mussels, & too much more … Freshly caught fish not only ensures sustainability but also ensures that a premium product ends up on plate.

Ajooz Albahar seafood restaurant is a restaurant that specializes in seafood cuisine and other Steaks, Meat, Chicken dishes and Special Sushi & Japanese Cuisine. Dishes may include freshwater Life fish. Ajooz Albahar concept may focus upon the preparation and service of fresh seafood, as well as High Quality Fresh Meat, Mutton & Chicken. (We never serve frozen products). Ajooz Albahar Seafood Restaurant also provide retail sales of seafood that consumers take away to prepare, or bring their own fish, and we prepare it for them.

Ajooz Albahar Seafood Restaurant, main target is to build a Loyal Customer Base;

By focusing on each and every minor details for our customer’s needs and expectations and spend a huge efforts every minute, every day to go beyond our guests’ expectations from material & personal service.Therefore, we are not only targeting customers who primarily like seafood, or other International Dishes but, We mainly target those who is looking for extraordinary Food & Beverage Quality, as well as exceptional quality of service with CONSISTENCY, every second of the day. One way to guarantee our high-quality products is through a very strict monitor on suppliers. We constantly monitor the freshness and quality as well as taste of our unique dishes.We use high quality imported ingredients, to maintain our unique recipes & taste.

Ajooz Albahar Restaurant always aims and plans to keep our outlets distinctive in order to avoid a repetitive, commercial look. So each corner of our restaurant was given a unique feel by our designing Architects. In order to make you feel very comfortable, during dining experience with Ajooz Albahar.

ABOUT US

History of Young Company with big dreams:

Ajooz Albahar Seafood restaurant was a big dream of two close friends, whom used to spend hours and hours daily dining in various restaurants in UAE, but they never feel that, they are getting the proper value, either in food quality, service or value of their money spent.
They often used to share their dreams about having a restaurant brand that can really make it happen in UAE, Our beloved country, after many years of dreams; they decided to make it happen. They will make this restaurant brand, they won’t dream any more.Therefore, the two friends search for a very experienced person with same dream and Philosophy to start their project, and they found the right person, with more than 20 years of successful food & beverage experiences and that was the starting point for making the dream becomes TRUE.

The dream was not that easy to make it happen, we all faced so many difficulties on the way, but our resolve was unrelenting.
We decided to mix International & local Emirati taste in a unique Seafood concept, that’s real distinguished food to the industry, Staying true to our roots decided to venture into establishing a chain of seafood restaurants to further expand our F&B portfolio across UAE, MENA, GCC. Ajooz Albahar is creating a new Seafood Restaurant culture. This unique approach of gathering all customers’ needs & dreams in a fusion cuisine is set to change the way individuals consume & think about their meals. Each item on our menu tells a story of relationships with individuals. Ajooz Albahar seafood restaurant culture is innovative, fun and incorporates each aspect of seafood, meat and all other unique food & beverage varieties and its relation to our lives. This new culture respects tradition and recognizes it as one of the important elements on which it is based, while maintaining the importance of tradition.
Ajooz Albahar Restaurant has its own interpretation on existing seafood, our line of products allow each individual to experience the flavours and aromas of a variety of delectable creative hot & cold gourmet dishes selected very carefully to satisfy our premium seafood lovers from our customers.

Always seeking the ultimate seafood experience, the movement of fresh seafood aficionados calls for outstanding equipment to help you prepare and serve great food at all times. Ajooz Albahar restaurant uses the most sophisticated food preparation equipment, and best cooking methods to serve our clients a unique taste of freshly prepared seafood, We are proud to introduce Ajooz Albahar Restaurant exclusive creative seafood dishes, which will change your way of thinking about having seafood meal, We also have our own distinguished salads, cold & hot appetizers, soups, breakfast menu as well as distinguished fresh Sushi options.Beyond the quality of our products, resides the unique character of Ajooz Albahar Restaurant which combines nostalgia and a stimulating spirit together with a special emphasis on a warm, personal connection with customers and stable superior service.

Our Core Values

Ajooz Albahar Restaurant is dedicated to core values that define our company philosophy to create unique experiences, to enhance the community and to conduct ourselves with integrity in everything we do.

Guiding Principles

Responsibility
